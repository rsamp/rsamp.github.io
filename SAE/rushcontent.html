Why ΣAE?

  • Sigma Alpha Epsilon Ratings:
    • #1 Fraternity by the Gallup Poll
    • #1 Fraternity by Heller Survey
    • #1 Fraternity by Bantas for 35 years
    • #1 Fraternity by Playboy on 19 out of 20 rated campuses
    • #1 Fraternity for more than 53 years (out of 60) by the College Survey Bureau

  • ΣAE...
    • Has the greatest number of initiates of any International Greek Letter at over 295,000 men
    • Has 212 chapters in 49 states, 9 colonies and 175 alumni associations
    • Has raised more money for community service in the past year that any other Greek organization
    • Has a quarterly fraternity magazine, The Record, published continuously since 1880
    • Has a student load program to assist undergraduate brothers with their education
    • Has an awards program to recognize outstanding contributions of Sigma Alpha Epsilon alumni, undergraduates, chapters, and others to the development of individuals and to the betterment of life in their communicty
    • Offers a scholarship program and has loaned out more than $20 million to students
    • Was the first International Fraternity to be established in the deep south, having been founded at the University of Alabama, Tuscaloosa, on March 9, 1856
    • Was the first fraternity to have an International Headquarters (Evanston, Illinois) and the first to build its own headquarters building (The Levere Memorial Temple), which is the only fraternity headquarters to have a chapel
    • Was the first fraternity to establish a leadership school (1935) that now has over 32,000 graduates
    • Was the first fraternity to offer a toll-free hazing hotline available for all members, pledges, and parents
    • Encourages pledge programs which enhance each individual's personal growth

    • 75% of the members of the U.S. Congress are Greek

    • 100 of 158 of those who have served on the President's Cabinet this century are/were Greek
